Pampoenkoekies / Pumpkin Fritters

Description

I found this recipe here.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500ml) cooked and drained pumpkin
  • 1 cup (250ml) self-raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) baking powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) sugar
  • oil for frying
  • Cinnamon sugar, syrup or honey for serving

Steps

  1. Mix together the pumpkin, flour, baking powder, salt, butter, egg, and sugar well.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons (30ml) oil in a frying pan and drop spoonfulls of the batter in the oil.
  3. Fry the fritters until golden brown on both sides.
  4. The pan shouldn't be too hot, or the outsides of the fritter will burn and the insides will be uncooked.
  5. Serve the fritters with cinnamon sugar, syrup or honey
  6. Enjoy!
