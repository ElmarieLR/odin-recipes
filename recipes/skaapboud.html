Skaapboud / Leg of Lamb

Description

I found this recipe here.

Ingredients

  • 1 leg of lamb (approximately 2kg)
  • 60 ml olive oil
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • salt and freshly grounded pepper
  • 6-8 large potatoes, peeled

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 140°C.
  2. Place leg of lamb in roasting pan.
  3. Pour the olive oil and lemon juice over the meat.
  4. Season the meat with salt and pepper
  5. Place in the oven and roast uncovered for 6 hours.
  6. Turn the oven's temperature up to 160°C.
  7. Arrange the potatoes around the meat and spoon the pan juices over them.
  8. Continue roasting for another 2 hours, turning the potatoes over after 1 hour.
  9. If the pan is starting to look dry, add a bit of water or white wine.
  10. Enjoy!
